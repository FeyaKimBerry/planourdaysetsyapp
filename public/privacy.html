Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

Planourdays ("we", "us", "the app") is a wedding planning application. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. We have designed Planourdays to keep your data private and under your control.

What information the app handles

Planourdays lets you record wedding planning details, including: partner names, your wedding date and venue, a budget with categories and expenses, a task checklist, a guest list (names, RSVP status, party size, meal and group choices), vendor contacts and payments, seating arrangements, and any photos you choose to add.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in two places, both of which belong to you:

  • Your device: the app saves your plan in your web browser's local storage so it works quickly and offline.
  • Your own Google Drive (optional): if you choose to sign in with Google, the app saves a single copy of your plan to a private, hidden application folder in your personal Google Drive, so your plan is backed up and can sync across your devices.

We do not operate a server that stores your wedding data, and your data is not sent to us or to any third party. It stays on your device and, if you sign in, in your own Google Drive account.

How the app uses Google services and your Google data

If you choose to connect Google Drive, Planourdays requests a single, limited permission:

Google Drive Application Data (drive.appdata) — this gives the app access only to its own hidden application data folder in your Google Drive. The app uses it solely to save and load your wedding plan file.

This permission does not allow Planourdays to see, open, read, edit, delete, or download any of your other Google Drive files, photos, or documents. The application data folder is hidden from your normal Drive view and is used only by Planourdays.

Limited Use disclosure

Planourdays's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only use Google Drive access to provide and improve the wedding-plan sync feature you requested.
  • We do not transfer or sell this data to third parties, except as needed to provide the feature, comply with law, or as part of a merger.
  • We do not use this data for advertising.
  • No humans read your Google data, except where you give consent for support, where required by law, or for security purposes.

Continuing without signing in

Signing in with Google is optional. You can use Planourdays without signing in, in which case your data is stored only in your browser on your current device and is not synced or backed up.

Removing access and deleting your data

You can disconnect Planourdays from your Google account at any time at your Google Account permissions page. You can clear locally stored data by clearing your browser data for this site. You may delete the app's data file from your Google Drive by removing app data through your Google account settings.

Children

Planourdays is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact
