Your data & Google

Your data stays yours

Planourdays is built to keep your information private and under your control. We do not run a server that stores your wedding data, and we never sell it.

🔒 Least-privilege Google access
Optional Google Drive sync uses a single, limited permission — drive.appdata — which lets Planourdays read and write only its own hidden application-data folder in your Google Drive. It is used solely to save and load your wedding-plan file.
  • ✓Planourdays cannot see, open, read, edit, delete or download any of your other Drive files, photos or documents.
  • ✓The app-data folder is hidden from your normal Drive view and is used only by Planourdays.
  • ✓Your plan is stored on your device and, if you sign in, in your own Google Drive — never on our servers.
  • ✓We adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Your Google data is never used for advertising or sold to third parties.
  • ✓You can disconnect access anytime from your Google Account permissions.
